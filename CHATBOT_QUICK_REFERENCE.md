# 💬 Chatbot Quick Reference

## What Is It?

A floating AI assistant (💬 icon in bottom-right) that:
- Guides users through the platform
- Suggests offers based on what users want
- Searches products by category
- Answers common questions

## Common Questions

### Q: How do I close the chat?
**A:** Click the [×] button in top-right of chat window or click the 💬 bubble again.

### Q: Can I search multiple things?
**A:** Yes! Keep asking questions. The bot remembers your conversation.

### Q: Will it show me all offers?
**A:** No, it shows top matches. Ask "Show more" for additional results.

### Q: Can I post offers through the bot?
**A:** Not yet! Use the dashboard form. Bot helps you FIND offers.

### Q: What if the bot doesn't understand?
**A:** Rephrase with different keywords. The bot learns from context.

### Q: Is my chat private?
**A:** Chat doesn't leave your browser. No server logging of conversations.

### Q: Can I search by location?
**A:** Coming soon! For now, search by category or type.

### Q: How often does the bot update offers?
**A:** Updates when you open chat. Fetches latest from database.

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Chat bubble doesn't appear | Refresh page • Check browser console |
| Can't send messages | Type and press Enter OR click Send button |
| Bot doesn't respond | Check internet connection • Try simpler query |
| Offers not showing | Ensure offers exist in system • Try different search |
| Chat window closes | Is normal • Click bubble to reopen |
| Slow responses | Wait for bot to think • Avoid too many messages |
