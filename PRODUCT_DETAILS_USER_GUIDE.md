# 🎯 How to Use Product-Specific Details Feature

## For Users

### Step-by-Step Guide

#### 1. **Navigate to Post New Offer**
   - Open the dashboard
   - Look for "📝 Post New Offer" panel on the right side

#### 2. **Select a Category**
   Choose from:
   - ⌚ Watch
   - 🏍️ Bike/Motorcycle
   - 🚗 Car/Vehicle
   - 📚 Books
   - 👔 Fashion/Clothing
   - 📱 Gadgets/Electronics
   - 🏠 Land/Property
   - 💎 Jewelry
   - 🎁 Other

#### 3. **Product Details Appear** 
   Once you select a category, specific fields automatically appear:
   
   **Example: If you select "Gadgets"**
   ```
   📋 Product Details
   ├── 🏷️ Brand: Apple
   ├── 📝 Model: iPhone 13 Pro
   ├── 📱 Type: Smartphone
   ├── ✨ Condition: Excellent
   └── ✅ Warranty Status: Active
   ```

#### 4. **Fill All Required Fields**
   - Fill in "What You Need"
   - Fill in "What You Offer"
   - Add Product Details (auto-generated based on category)
   - Upload required documents
   - Add image URL (optional)

#### 5. **Submit Offer**
   - Click "📤 Post Offer" button
   - Your offer with detailed product information is now live!

## Troubleshooting

**Q: I selected a category but no product details appear?**
A: Refresh the page or try selecting a different category first, then reselect your category.

**Q: Can I change product details after posting?**
A: Not yet - create a new offer with corrected details.

**Q: What if my product doesn't fit any category?**
A: Select "🎁 Other" category - it doesn't require specific details.

**Q: Are product details mandatory?**
A: Product details are auto-generated for each category. Fill what's relevant to your product.
